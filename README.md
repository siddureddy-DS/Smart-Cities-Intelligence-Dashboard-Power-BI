# SMART CITIES INTELLIGENCE DASHBOARD

## BANL 6600 – Power BI Final Project

An advanced multi-dimensional Power BI dashboard analyzing smart city performance, sustainability, environmental health, mobility, safety, and livability across 40+ global cities from 2022 to 2024.

---

## PROJECT OVERVIEW

This dashboard integrates multiple Smart City and Sustainability datasets, including Smart City Index scores, air quality (PM2.5), CO2 emissions, demographic indicators, and renewable energy share, to provide a holistic view of urban performance.

By combining environmental, economic, and technological dimensions, the model enables deeper insights than traditional single-metric city rankings.

The project includes advanced data preparation such as:
- Aligning mismatched timeframes
- Resolving inconsistent city names
- Normalizing different units of measurement
- Creating a custom Sustainability Index
- Developing quartile-based classifications

These enhancements allow cities to be compared fairly across multiple dimensions and time periods.

**Central Research Question:**
*Are Smart Cities truly sustainable, or is there a gap between innovation and environmental outcomes?*
